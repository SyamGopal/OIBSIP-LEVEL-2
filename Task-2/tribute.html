Tribute to Senior NTR

Remembering the legendary actor and politician

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao, popularly known as Senior NTR, was a legendary actor, director, and politician who left an indelible mark on Indian cinema and politics. Born on 28 May 1923 in Nimmakuru, Andhra Pradesh, he started his career as a stage actor before making his film debut in 1949 with the Telugu film Palletoori Pilla. He went on to act in over 300 films in a career spanning more than four decades, and won numerous awards and accolades for his performances.

Senior NTR was not only a great actor, but also a great human being who had a deep concern for the welfare of his fellow citizens. In 1982, he founded the Telugu Desam Party (TDP) with the aim of serving the people of Andhra Pradesh and promoting Telugu language and culture. He went on to become the Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh three times, and implemented many progressive policies that transformed the state.

Senior NTR's contributions to Indian cinema and politics will always be remembered and cherished. He was a true legend who inspired generations of actors and politicians, and his legacy continues to live on.
